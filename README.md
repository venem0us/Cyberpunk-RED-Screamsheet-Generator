# Cyberpunk RED Screamsheet Generator
**Screamsheets** entertain, inform, and mislead Night City's masses — and give GMs a powerful tool to deliver information, advance plot, and show consequences. Not a journalist? No problem. The Cyberpunk RED Screamsheet Generator helps you craft in-world articles to hand straight to your players. 

Just download the HTML file to your computer and open in your web browser of choice.
